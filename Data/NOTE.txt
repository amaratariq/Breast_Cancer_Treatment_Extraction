Place cancer registry data here if evaluation, training, or finetuning is needed. The file must have the following information columns

[ 	'Clinic #', 
	'Surg, Date Most Def Tx',
        'Surg, Date of Tx, Hist',
        'Rad, Summ Date Start',
        'Rad, Summ Date End',
        'Chemo, Summ Date Start',
        'Chemo, Summ Date End',
        'Hormone, Summ Date Started',
        'Immuno, Summ Date Start' 	]


Place files containing clinical notes in subfolder named "clinical_notes".
